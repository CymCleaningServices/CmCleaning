About Us

Cleaning Done With Care & Pride

C&M Cleaning Services LLC is a locally owned and operated cleaning company based in Algona, WA. With 9+ years of experience, we deliver superior cleaning for homes, offices, and post-construction properties throughout the Greater Seattle area.

Whether it's your weekly house clean, a deep scrub after renovation, or ongoing office maintenance — our trained team brings the same attention to detail every single visit. We are a Nextdoor Neighborhood Favorite in Algona, Kent, and Puyallup, and trusted by contractors and homeowners alike.
